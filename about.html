The Founder

A Rare Blend of Global Operational Expertise

The Founder & Principal Advisor of Zenith Corporation brings a unique combination of global operational expertise, maritime logistics, and strategic consultancy to clients across industries. With experience spanning multinational corporate environments, complex shipping operations, and hospitality consulting, they specialize in transforming unstructured businesses into scalable, efficient operations.
