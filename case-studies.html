Hospitality · Feasibility Study

Upscale Resort Feasibility & Brand Positioning

Key Result

Client received a clear roadmap for operator selection, area program, and investment planning — identifying an upper-upscale market gap for a 150-room development.

A regional developer wanted to build a 150-room premium resort but was uncertain about market positioning and brand strategy. Zenith conducted a comprehensive market and financial feasibility study from the ground up.

  • Analyzed local hospitality supply, proposed guest room sizes, and amenity benchmarks
  • Identified an underserved gap in the upper-upscale segment and recommended suitable brand affiliations
  • Provided brand positioning recommendations and operator selection criteria
  • Delivered an investor-ready report for architects, financiers, and project stakeholders

03
Real Estate · Financial Structuring

Financial Structuring for Hospitality Investment

Key Result

Projected IRR stabilized at 17% through book-value restructuring, enabling successful term loan financing for the development.

A private investment group faced high land market value that depressed projected returns on a hotel development. Zenith advised on restructuring the financial model using the land's book value, fundamentally improving investment viability.

  • Built dynamic scenario planning models to stress-test multiple financial structures
  • Automated sensitivity analyses on debt-to-equity ratios and land valuation scenarios
  • Recommended book-value restructuring to stabilize IRR at a bankable 17%
  • Supported the client through term loan financing preparation
Argus Enterprise Power BI
